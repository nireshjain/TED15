TED
IDEAS WORTH SPREADING
TED is a non profit organisation owned by the Sapling Foundation that started out in 1984, to bring together people from the three worlds: Technology, Entertainment, Design; upholding a strong belief that ideas have the power to change attitudes, lives and ultimately the world. With a goal that offers free knowledge and inspiration from the world's most inspired thinkers, and also a community of curious souls to engage with ideas and each other, it addresses a wide range of topics within the research and practice of science and culture in a way that makes them alluring. The speakers are given a maximum of 18 minutes to present their ideas in the most innovative and engaging ways.
