The award winning author, Chuck Palahniuk remarks in his novel Survivor, There are only patterns, patterns on top of patterns, patterns that affect other patterns, patterns hidden by patterns and patterns within patterns. If you watch close, history does nothing but repeat itself. What we call chaos is just patterns we have not recognized. What we call random is just patterns we cant decipher. What we cannot understand, we call non-sense. The pursuit of orderliness and order in the seemingly omnipresent chaos has indeed been a major driver in the perpetuation of the world. This year, TEDxIITBHU adopts the theme of Order in Chaos in line with TED framework of a generic topic yet simple in driving the point home. We thus hope to spike the quest for recognizing the patterns, deciphering the unknown and reducing the non-sense. Varanasi or Benares, now also the PM's constituency provides an ideal setting to uncover the order in chaos, much like its populace in the chaotic "gullies".
